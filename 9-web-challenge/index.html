Does critic points even matter in wine?

We often use scores from renown wine critic such as Robert Parker to evaluate the quality of a wine. When it comes to price, does these scores affect the price? If so, by how much? And is there a variation between different countries?

The project is to test the hypothesis that there is a statistically significant relationship between the critic points and price of a particular wine.
